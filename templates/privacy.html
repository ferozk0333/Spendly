Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic information about how you interact with the app (e.g., pages visited).

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. Specifically:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To display your expense history and summaries.
  • To improve app performance and fix issues.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. You may delete your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for infrastructure (e.g., hosting). These providers are contractually obligated to handle your data securely and only as necessary to deliver their services. We do not integrate with advertising or analytics platforms that track you across websites.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information on our website. You may also review our Terms and Conditions.
